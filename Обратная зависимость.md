# Обратное опосредование

- Situation in which a person start to act opposite to some action that annoyed him
	- For example, your father have got divorced and you promised yourself that you never do so
		- In adulthood you will restrict yourself and wont get divorce even if you need it
			- Prohibition on divorces become a [[Глубинные убеждения]]
		- You think you have separated from your father, but you havent. You still depends on his actions, but you acting opposite to it - [[сепарация от родителей]]
- This effect hinders rational ([[рациональность]]) decision making
- May be [[Реактивное сопротивление]] can take part in this effect
