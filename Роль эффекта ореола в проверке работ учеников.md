# Роль эффекта ореола в проверке работ учеников

- When you are checking some work of your student ([[Тестирование в педагогике]]), the correctness of the first answer very strongly influence on the marking following answers
	- Because of the [[Эффект ореола]]
		- If the first answer was perfectly correct, you mark following ambiguous or not fully correct answer in favour of student
- Is students use methaphor in the first good answer, will it change teachers work even more? - [[Метафора изменяет всю дальнейшую информацию]]
