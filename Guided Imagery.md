# Guided Imagery

- Technique to cope with [[стресс]]
	- Type of [[Медитация]] with visualisation
- Once you get to a relaxed state, begin to envision yourself in the midst of the most relaxing environment you can imagine
	- try to involve all of your senses. What does it look like? How does it feel? What special scents are involved? Do you hear the roar of a fire, the splash of a waterfall, or the sounds of chipper birds?
	- You may want to use ambient sounds that compliment your imagery
