# Таксономия SOLO

- Для оценки результата обучения

1. Prestructural: Unstructured, inappropriate work. 
2. Unistructural: Appropriate presentation of one relevant subject aspect.
3. Multistructural: Appropriate presentation of several relevant but unconnected subject aspects.
4. Relational: Integration of several relevant subject aspects.
5. Extended Abstract: Creation of a coherent, holistic approach at a new abstraction level
