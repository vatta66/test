# Предметный и логический подходы к решению задач

- In [[План урока на день науки]] students werent try to solve [[ситуационная задача]] because they thought that they didnt know biology, and therefore they couldnt solve biology problem - [[Решение проблемы]]
	- Its a subject-based approach
- We need to teach them logic-based approach to solving problems
	- Because every [[ситуационная задача]] in our lesson could only be solved by logic([[логическое мышление]])
	- In logic-based approach student try to find an answer using his logic
		- He analyse meaningful sides of problem, ask some [[Вопросы]] and so on
- In subject-based approach students try to find some knowledges associated with the subject (like [[семантическая репрезентация]])
	- For example, i am asked some question about bacteria. I try to find some knowledges associated with bacteria, and very often i dont find such a knowledge
		- Maybe because I think i am techi ([[Гуманитариев и технарей не существует]])
			- And very often i dont even try to find an answer because i have a strong attitude of ignorance toward my knowledge of a subject or an attitude toward my predisposition to the subject
	- This approach instil [[Установка на данность]] to students
- Cause
	- Every subjects are isolated
		- Knowledges level are low - the very bottom of [[Таксономия SOLO]]
	- [[Гуманитариев и технарей не существует]]
- Solutions
	- [[Проблемно-комплексная теория]]
	- Teach students [[логическое мышление]]
	- [[Нужно учить школьников задавать вопросы]]
