# Глубинные убеждения

- Core beliefs is what our thoughts (especially negative - [[Негативные эмоции]]) are based on
	- Some [[Когнитивная модель|автоматические мысли]] arise because of core beliefs
	- Core beliefs are deeply ingrained in our mind
		- It become the unconscious way of reasoning
- It influence on our [[Внимание]]
	- For example, [[Предвзятость подтверждения]] - we only notice the information that proove our core belief (im ugly, Jack is successful and im not etc)
		- You reinforce your core belief according to the [[Положительное подкрепление]]
			- The [[поведенческий эксперимент]] might wont work
- To recognize that core thoughts we must firstly learn how to decect our automatic thoughts
	- Then, during their analisis, you can reveal the more deep belief that have caused your thought
- To detect your core belief you need to use [[Техника вертикального спуска для выявления глубинных убеждений]]
- Might the [[Установка на рост]] be a core belief?
