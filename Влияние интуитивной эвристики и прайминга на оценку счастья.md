# Влияние интуитивной эвристики и прайминга на оценку счастья

- If we use [[прайминг]] effect with [[эвристики]], we can [[Манипуляция]] peoples attitudes toward [[счастье]]
- If we ask them firstly how happy they are now, and then how many dates have you had on the last month, we will see no correlation
	- But if we ask firstly about dates, and then about happiness, we will see that those who have more dates are happier than those who have less [1] - [2]
		- In this case heuristic question on the question “how happy are you now” become question “how satisfied am I now?”
			- Because those who recall more dates direct their [[внимание]] on positive side of their life - [[Позитивные эмоции]], and those who recall less fell [[Негативные эмоции]] about it [3]
				- [[Предвзятость подтверждения]] might take place here
				- Also, it partly proves my definition of [[Счастье]]: if you can recall many eudaemonic pleasures like dates, you will experience more satisfaction in moment
