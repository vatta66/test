# Эффект затрат на переключение

- Switching between tasks can devour up to 40% of your [[продуктивность]] [1]
- Consequences
	- Significantly decreas the quality of your work
	- Impair [[encoding]] of information
		- Study show that switching between tasks hinders [[Память|remembering]] [2]
	- Distract [[Внимание]]
	- Impede [[Состояние потока]] entrance
	- Leads to [[Принятие решения]] fatigue
- Despite this, if you need to [[Решение проблемы]] but you are stuck, switch a task might be a great idea 
- Solutions
	- [[Медитация]]
	- [[Interstitial journaling]]
