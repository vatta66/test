# Ассертивность

- Assertiveness is the ability to express your feelings and assert your rights while respecting the feelings and rights of others
- Assertive people tend to have fewer [[Социальный конфликт]] in their dealings with others [1]
	- Less [[стресс]] in everyday life
- Positively assosiated with [[высокая самооценка]] [2]
