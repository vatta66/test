# Дыхательные техники

- Counted breathing
	- Inhale (nose) - 5 sec, exhale (mouth) - 8 sec
	- 4-7-8 breathing. Hold breathing on inhale on 7 sec. After 4 sycles make 8 ordinary breath
- Use of [[Guided Imagery]] to release [[стресс]]
	- As you inhale, imagine that all the stress in your body is coming from your extremities and into your chest. Then, as you exhale, imagine that the stress is leaving your body through your breath and dissipating right in front of you
- Alternate Nostril Breathing
	- As you inhale, place your finger over your right nostril. On the exhalex hold left nostril. You can use counted breathing
