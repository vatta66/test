# Outcome bias

- One of the [[Когнитивные искажения]]
- Says that people tend to evaluate actions in terms of results
	- They can change [[продуктивность]] and [[рациональность|rational]] strategy just because it doesnt give desirable results
		- But it is the best strategy in long term, and it might need a little more attempts
	- Also, people prone to evaluate the actions of other people according to it results, but not to their intentions
		- It can be a possibility of [[Манипуляция]]
	- When people see a good result, they will think that all way to that result was right
		- Although [[После не значит вследствие]]
			- If someone won the war, it doesnt mean that he had made the best decisions
		- The good result is a [[Положительное подкрепление]] for person to act the same way next time
			- Thus it can become a superstition - [[Работа суеверий]]
- Related - [[Фундаментальная ошибка атрибуции]]
- According to my hypothesis, this is because [[Мозг по природе консеквенциален]]
