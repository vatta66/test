# Demand characteristics

- Form of [[Reactivity in psychology]]
- In the beginning of an experiment participants form an interpretation of the experiment and change their behavior to fit that interpretation
	- Individual can try to deceive experimentation and make wrong and dishonest answers
- How to reduce demand characteristics
	- We can deceive participants about [[гипотеза в исследовании]]
	- Conduct the [[Дизайн эксперимента в психологии#Дебрифинг]]
	- Dont inform participants about hypothesis of the experiment - [[Эффект ожидания наблюдателя|observer-expectancy effect]]
