# На удовлетворенность влияют факторы, находящиеся в фокусе внимания

- The [[Ощущающее и вспоминающее я|experiencing self]] is contributed more in activity estimation than the [[Ощущающее и вспоминающее я|remembering self]]
- Workplace mood has little to do with factors that affect job satisfaction as a whole [1]
	- Much more important instant satisfaction factors ex.
		- Level of [[шум]] and another irritation and [[стресс]]-factors
		- Ability to communicate ([[Потребность во взаимосвязи с другими людьми]])
		- Presence of a boss
		- Hecticness of a schedule
- The key point is [[Внимание]]
	- You always prone to the influence of instant factors, so your attention is always directed on these factors
	- But if you start focusing your attention on factors of overall satisfaction, you can be satisfied with them too
		- These factors represent “satisfaction in future” from my [[счастье]] definition, whereas instant satisfaction represent “satisfaction in moment”
			- You need both of them
		- Factors of overall satisfaction are often overestimated because of an [[Иллюзия фокусировки]] effect
- While your car is new for you, its an instant satisfaction of activity factor, but when you get used to it, it becomes general satisfaction of activity factor and dont give you satisfaction passively
	- So the [[Эффект гедонистической адаптации]] can take place here
- If you prefer [[общение]] to another factor, you will be able to receive satisfaction bigger amount of time
	- Because good communication will always be in focus of your attention, whereas some kind of material pleasure such as car of phone not ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/LU9XSCWF?page=415&annotation=Z39ZVVTH))
