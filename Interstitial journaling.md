# Interstitial journaling

- [[продуктивность]]
- This method can help to reduce [[Прокрастинация]]
- Also you can learn how long do some activities take to be done
	- It can help you to avoid [[Ошибка планирования]]
- A brief period of writing out what have you just done can give you time for switching your task context, like in [[Эффект затрат на переключение|switching cost effect]]
- Also this type of journaling can be a [[Практики осознанности]]
- Simple way
	- Every time you start new task, you should write
		- Current time
		- Just finished
		- Next tasks (and just tasks you have remembered about)
- When you get used to it, you can add more [[Рефлексия]]
	- How i feel?
	- How this task bring me closer to my goal?
	- Wether i concentrated easy or not?
		- Why?
	- What can i improve next time
