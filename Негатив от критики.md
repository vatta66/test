# Негатив от [[Критика]]

- If you feel [[негативные эмоции]] when someone criticises you, you need to
	- Dispose of negative emotions - [[Способы избавиться от негатива в моменте]]
	- Evaluate the priority of change - [[определение приоритета изменения]]
		- Make a decision whether you will change or not
	- If yes, you need to say yourself that this criticize is all for your [[Развитие личности]]  - [[Установка В этом мое развитие]]
		- You need to believe that this little or even big mistake you were criticized about can help you to become better
			- When you become confident about it, write or say it to the other person, and if they really want you to become better, they will encourage and stand by you until you truly become better
		- Можно представить себя без этого плохого качества за которое тебя критиковали, представить что еще улучшится, чтобы себя замотивировать, разработать план, как ты будешь от него избавляться и начать, то есть главный акцент на то, что ты с помощью этого станешь лучше
	- If no and if someone who have criticized you is important for you, you need to discuss the situation with him and find solution that will satisfy both of you
		- Ex. just say that you experience a lot of [[Позитивные эмоции]] when doing something and you dont want to change it, and the other person, if they really care about you, understand you and will feel pleasant because you are happy doing it
			- Or you can help the other person find reasons why something you do displease them, and help to work with it - [[Помогай другим людям развиваться]] and [[Психологическая работа]]
	- And if you dont want to change yourself and someone who have criticized you isnt important to you, you need just stop care about it
		- In other case it would be complicated, but you have just used priority evaluation which is quite similar to [[Когнитивная реструктуризация]], so it will be easily for you to do it
			- But you still need to write it out or say it out loud, ex “this person isnt important to me, i dont care about them and there are nothing for my self-development here, and i dont want to change myself because i love this part of me” and so on, you can make this sentence more personal for you
