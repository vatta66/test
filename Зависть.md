# Зависть

- One of the [[Негативные эмоции]]
- Although envy seems almost like a compliment, it can be quite dehumanizing. It reduces the object of envy to something very narrow and masks the full picture of who they are and what their life is like
- Way of coping
	- Wrong way
		- “Sure, he has a nice car, but I’m better looking” is not going to get you very far. You might feel vindicated in the moment, but sooner or later someone is going to come along who has a nicer car than you and is better looking.
	- Practice [[Эмпатия]]: When you find yourself envying someone, try to put yourself in their shoes. Think about what they might be going through, and how they might be feeling. This can help you see things from a different perspective and reduce your feelings of envy.
- Зависть может быть результатом [[Когнитивный диссонанс]]
